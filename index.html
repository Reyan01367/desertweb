About Us

Born From a Passion for the Desert

Desert Drive was inspired by a deep love for desert adventures in Saudi Arabia. We noticed that many people want to explore the desert but struggle to find suitable vehicles and organized safari services.

Through extensive research and creative thinking, we designed complete desert safari packages that include safe vehicles, professional drivers, and additional services to enhance the overall experience.

Our mission aligns with Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030, promoting tourism growth while ensuring customer safety and environmental responsibility.

Your Safety

Safety is Our Top Priority

Every Desert Drive safari follows a comprehensive safety protocol developed from UAE tourism regulations and international best practices.

👨‍✈️

Certified Drivers

All drivers are professionally trained and certified for desert terrain driving with years of experience.

🔧

Vehicle Inspection

Every vehicle undergoes a thorough pre-trip inspection including tyre pressure, engine, and safety equipment checks.

🚑

Emergency Equipment

All vehicles carry first aid kits, GPS tracking, satellite phones for remote areas, and fire extinguishers.

📋

Safety Briefings

Every passenger receives a comprehensive safety briefing before departure, covering protocols and emergency procedures.

🛞

Sand-Ready Tyres

Tyres are professionally deflated for optimal sand performance and re-inflated before returning to paved roads.
